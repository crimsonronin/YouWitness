YouWitness

YouWitness is an online eyewitness identification experiment. The purpose is to examine line-up circumstances for accurate eyewitness identification. The test will take no longer than 5-10 minutes. Before continuing, please read the information below.

By clicking the 'I Accept' button, you are consenting to the conditions listed below. Any concerns or questions please email researcher Alyssa Sbisa.

Conditions

It is required that you are over 18 years of age and can sufficiently read English.

The test should take no longer than 5-10 minutes, however you are permitted to take as much time as you like.

You have the right to withdraw from further participation at any stage along the test, however the data you have entered will not be recorded or analysed.

There are no risks associated with this research, and your answers will be anonymous and confidential.

At the conclusion of the test, you'll be provided with the option to enter your email address to be informed of the research results when they are released in October 2013. Alternatively, you can click here to submit your interest.

Procedure

  • Nominate your sex: male or female
  • View a 45 second video
  • Play a timed (60 second) word puzzle
  • View a suspect line-up
  • Rate your level of confidence in your selection
