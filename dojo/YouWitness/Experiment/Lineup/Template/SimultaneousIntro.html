Suspect Lineup

You will now take part in the eyewitness identification task. Your task is to identify the perpetrator within the previously viewed video.
Please note the perpetrator MAY or MAY NOT be within the lineup.

It is important you read the following before clicking 'Next':

  • You can take as much time as you'd like.
  • You will view all 6 suspects at the same time.
  • You can only choose 1 suspect as the perpetrator.
  • If you think the perpetrator is not within the lineup, select the 'Perpetrator not present' option.
